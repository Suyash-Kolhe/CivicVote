# CivicPulse: Election Education Prototype

## Problem Statement
Election processes are often complex, intimidating, and poorly understood by the general public. This prototype aims to demystify the process through an interactive, step-by-step assistant.

## Features
- **Voter Journey Timeline**: A visual breakdown of the phases of an election cycle.
- **CivicPulse AI**: A Gemini-powered assistant to answer specific questions about voter registration, laws, and election day.
- **Interactive Checklists**: Personalized steps to ensure a user is "Election Ready".
- **Key Dates Tracker**: A countdown to major election milestones.

## Design
- **Recipe**: Combination of "Recipe 9 (Oversized Typographic)" for the timeline and "Recipe 8 (Clean Utility)" for the interface.
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (Serif) for authority, Inter (Sans) for clarity.
- **Palette**: Navy (#1a365d), Vibrant Red (#ef4444), and Clean Slate (#f8fafc).
